This election is officially announced by Chief Election Commisioner Mr.Nasim Zaidi to elect the Prime Minister of India . India is the first country in the world which has adopted online voting system to be used for government election.



Notice for voter:

  • Voter are supposed to register before voting.
    Voter should not disclose his/her id and password to any other individual.
  • Voter should specify username same printed in as aadhar card.



Notice for candidate:

  • Candidate should upload his details to election commissioner with valid documents.
  • Candidate details will be verified by election commissoner and added as a candidate(if all documents are right).
